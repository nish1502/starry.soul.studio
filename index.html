meet winoliya★

hi, i am winoliya, and i have always been an avid lover of art. whether it was admiring the crochet wall hangings in my grandmother's home, pausing to appreciate the whimsical illustrations on a delivery bag, or finding joy in a thoughtfully designed menu at a quaint café, art has always found its way into my life.

i initially started this account simply to share my sketches, little creations, and all the cool things i loved making. over time, this space became so much more than just an account—it became a place where i could share pieces of myself.

and now, after more than a year of endless musing, i am overjoyed to spread that same love by opening my little dream: starry soul studio.

at starry soul studio, i'll be creating and selling clay magnets, keychains, postcards, and so much more. and if you have something special in mind, custom orders are always welcome. i truly hope you'll love it as much as i do.

with love and gratitude, winoliya
